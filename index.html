About Me

Everyone who comes into this page! Hello! I'm Su yeon Kim , and I'm majoring in communication design at Sangmyung University.
I am very interested in graphic design, UX/UI, and web design, and I am studying this subject passionately. My Design style is a point.
I prefer design with a little fun elements to boring in any project.
For example, add points with colors, or add fun to the design by using graphic elements. Also, I like to meet various people and study together.
I am an extrovert and considerate person, so I get along well with others and adapt well to the community! I get energy when I'm with people lol If you want to communicate with me like this..
